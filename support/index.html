Installation

Install Coffee Home from the Google Workspace Marketplace using the listing associated with your Google Workspace account. If your organization manages add-on installation, ask your Google Workspace administrator to approve Coffee Home.

Using the Meet add-on

Open Google Meet, choose Coffee Home from the Meet sidebar, sign in with your Google account, and use the workspace to manage notes, prompts, and follow-up reminders for the meeting.

Common issues

  • Add-on does not appear: confirm the add-on is installed and enabled for your Google Workspace account.
  • Authorization fails: confirm you are using the intended Google account and retry authorization.
  • Unable to sign in: clear stale browser sessions or contact support with the error message.
  • Organization administrator blocks installation: ask your administrator to approve the Marketplace app.

Account and data deletion

Email support@builditfaster.com to request account deletion or deletion of Coffee Home data associated with your Google account.
