Google Meet add-on

Coffee Home

Coffee Home is a Google Meet add-on that helps users keep lightweight meeting notes, shared prompts, and follow-up reminders close at hand during calls.

Install from Google Workspace Marketplace Sign in / Open app

How it works

  1. Install the add-on from Google Workspace Marketplace.
  2. Open it from the Google Meet sidebar during a meeting.
  3. Sign in and use Coffee Home to capture notes, prompts, and follow-up reminders.

What Google data it uses

Coffee Home requests access to basic Google account identifiers, including name, email address, and profile image, solely to authenticate users and show the correct workspace inside the Meet add-on.

Coffee Home does not sell Google user data or use it for advertising.
