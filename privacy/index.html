Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Last updated: June 15, 2026

1. Who operates the service

Coffee Home is operated by Build It Faster, a United States-based developer. Contact us at support@builditfaster.com.

2. Information collected

  • Account information, such as name and email address.
  • Google account identifiers, such as Google user ID and profile image.
  • Google Meet add-on context needed to display the app during a meeting.
  • User-submitted content, such as notes, prompts, and follow-up items.
  • Billing data if paid plans are enabled.
  • Technical logs, IP address, device, browser, and diagnostic data.

3. Google user data

Coffee Home accesses basic Google account identifiers, including name, email address, profile image, and Google user ID, to authenticate users and display the correct workspace in the Google Meet sidebar. Coffee Home may access Google Meet add-on context solely to render the in-meeting experience.

Coffee Home stores account identifiers while an account remains active. User-submitted content is retained until the user deletes it or requests deletion. Google user data is not shared with advertisers or sold. Users can request deletion by emailing support@builditfaster.com.

Coffee Home's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

4. How information is used

We use information to provide the Meet add-on, authenticate users, save user-submitted meeting content, respond to support requests, maintain security, debug issues, and improve reliability.

5. Data sharing and subprocessors

We may share data with service providers that host the application, process support requests, process payments, or provide security and diagnostics. These providers may process data only as needed to deliver services for Coffee Home.

6. Data retention and deletion

Account data is retained while the account is active. Technical logs are retained for a limited period needed for security, debugging, and compliance. Users may request account and data deletion by contacting support.

7. Security measures

We use HTTPS, access controls, least-privilege permissions, logging, and operational review practices designed to protect user data.

8. User choices and rights

Users may update account information, revoke Google authorization from their Google account, delete user-submitted content where supported, or request access, correction, export, or deletion by contacting support.

9. Children's privacy

Coffee Home is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Policy changes

We may update this policy as the service changes. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact information

Email support@builditfaster.com for privacy, support, or deletion requests.
